Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited is a British luxury automobile
headquarters are located on the Goodwood Estate in Goodwood, West Sussex, England, United Kingdom
From 1906 to 2003, motor cars were manufactured and marketed under the Rolls-Royce brand by Rolls-Royce Motors
